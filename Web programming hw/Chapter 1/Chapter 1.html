Biryani

This dish is famous in many parts of Asia and the subcontinent

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 4 tablespoons ghee
  • Salt to taste

How to prepare

  1. Wash the rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee in a large pot and sauté the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are no longer pink.
  5. Stir in the tomatoes, yogurt, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the soaked rice and enough water to cover the rice by about an inch. Bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat to low, cover the pot, and cook until the rice is tender and the chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala, cilantro, and mint leaves over the biryani before serving.
